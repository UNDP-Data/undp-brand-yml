Abstract

This Quarto document is designed to illustrate how the branding file affects the look and feel of documents across several formats. It contains a mix of placeholder text (lorem ipsum) and HDI-related content for illustration purposes only. It is designed to provide an example of how various elements of a Quarto document, such as images, tables, figures, references, are branded. Refer to undp-data/undp-brand-yml repository for more details about the package.
